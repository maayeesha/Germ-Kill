## 🎮 About the Game

**Germ-Kill** is an action-packed arcade game where you take on the role of a **doctor** defending against an invasion of harmful germs and viruses. Use your vaccination powers to eliminate enemies, avoid getting infected, and achieve the highest score possible!

Perfect for:
- 🎯 Casual gaming enthusiasts
- 👨‍💻 Python and Pygame learners
- 🧑‍🎓 Game development beginners
- 🎉 Quick entertainment breaks

---

## 🎯 Game Features

- 🏥 **Play as a Doctor** - Control your character and eliminate germs
- 🦠 **Enemy Germs** - Multiple enemies spawn and attack you
- 💉 **Vaccine Bullets** - Shoot down germs with vaccine projectiles
- 🎵 **Sound Effects** - Background music and hit/kill sound effects
- 🎨 **Visual Assets** - Custom sprites for player, enemies, and projectiles
- 📊 **Score Tracking** - Keep track of your kills and performance
- ⚡ **Fast-Paced Action** - Increasing difficulty as you progress
- 🔄 **Game States** - Play, lose, restart mechanics

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

### How to Play:

1. **Control your Doctor** - Move left and right to avoid germs
2. **Shoot Vaccines** - Fire projectiles to eliminate enemies
3. **Survive Waves** - Germs spawn continuously and get faster
4. **Maximize Score** - Earn points for every germ killed
5. **Game Over** - Protect yourself from being hit by germs

### Controls:

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **A** or **Left Arrow** | Move Left |
| **D** or **Right Arrow** | Move Right |
| **Space** or **Left Click** | Shoot Vaccine |
